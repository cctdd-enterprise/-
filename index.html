About Us – How It Works

At CCTDD Enterprise, our mission is to make international payments simple, transparent, and reliable for businesses. We understand the challenges companies face when trading across borders — from delayed payments to unnecessary friction. That’s why we’ve built a solution that empowers businesses to scale globally while keeping every transaction secure and compliant.

Invoice & Payment Creation

Businesses generate invoices or payment requests. Each invoice includes a secure payment link powered by our technology.

Regulated Payment Processing

Payments are processed by regulated providers (e.g., Stripe, Wise), ensuring rigorous financial and regulatory standards.

Direct Settlement to Businesses

Funds settle directly to the business account with the provider or bank. CCTDD never holds or controls client funds.

Our Role

We provide technology, reporting, and customer support for a seamless process. Revenue comes from service fees or subscriptions.

✅ Why This Matters

  • Transparency & Trust: We never handle funds directly, removing hidden risk.
  • Security & Compliance: Transactions are managed through regulated financial institutions.
  • Efficiency: Get paid faster with less hassle, improving cash flow and growth.
  • Global Reach: Tools that remove barriers to working with international clients.

Our Services

  • Seamless integration with regulated payment providers
  • Business support tailored for global expansion
  • Technology services built for security and compliance
